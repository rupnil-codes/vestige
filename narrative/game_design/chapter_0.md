
# Chapter 0: The Prologue

---

## Gameplay

> Nighttime, Cold, Middle of the Snowy Taiga, dense fog,
> snowing and the ground is covered in snow, thundering,
> sounds of wind and wild animals (owl, fox).
>
> The moon is bright enough to see ahead.
>
> _A bright warm cabin in sight._

1. Player blinks and stands up from an ominous bench in the middle.
2. Silhouettes are present some near the bench, some near the cabin, some throughout.
    - These "silhouette" do not move, just passively stand in their places.
    - They vanish with some particle effect when touched.
    - Each one vanishes with a breathing sound, and each one is a bit different.
3. Let the player soak in the atmosphere. After 2/3 seconds Instruct "Find them all."
4. Counter at the top of the screen counts the silhouettes found. (Like, "1 Found" the counter only appears after atleast one is found)
5. When the player reaches the cabin, it is unlocked.
6. The player opens the warm cabin door after wandering through a freezing foggy forest and sees a single silhouette standing silently at the far end of the room, already looking at them.
7. As the player moves towards it, the light flickers. They DO NOT reach it.
8. The door slams shut.
9. Light goes out with a flash. Whispers closing in.
10. Screen goes dark. Transitions to Chapter 1.
